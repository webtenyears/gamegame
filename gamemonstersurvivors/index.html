üé≤ How to Play Monster Survivors

Getting Started

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the battlefield. Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies, so focus on positioning and survival. Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters to level up.

Leveling Up

When you level up, choose from three random upgrade options. These can include new weapons, passive abilities, or improvements to existing skills. Each choice significantly impacts your playstyle and survival chances.

Survival Strategy

Stay mobile to avoid enemy attacks, prioritize collecting experience gems, and choose upgrades that complement each other. Some combinations create powerful synergies that can help you survive much longer against increasingly difficult waves.
